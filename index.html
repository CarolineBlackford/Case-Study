PROTO PERSONA

Before conducting interviews the UX team created a proto persona of a travel application user. Before the the research plan, as a team, we created a user that fit our target market.


AFFINITY DIAGRAM

As a team we wrote down our insights from our six interviews. From the the interviews and insights we categorized the information into a affinity diagram.


FEATURE PRIORITIZATION METHOD

After the I like, I wish, What if method was completed the designer then created a Feature Prioritization map, identifying the most important problems to solve first.


EMPATHY MAP

Each team member created a empathy map from the insight and affinity diagram content creating an outline of a user persona.

PERSONA

From the empathy map emerged a user persona. Savannah Smith is an excellent example of someone that would use our mobile application and has qualities of our target market.



PROBLEM STATEMENT

Our application was designed to achieve the creation of a drink from the user’s ingredients within their cabinets. We have observed that users need a convenient, fast, and easy to operate application to create a drink because they want a place to find fun and new drinks for themselves and their guests from ingredients they have at home. How might our application improve creating drink options from their cabinets so our users can generate a cheerful environment for themselves and their guests?


STORYBOARD

A Storyboard was used as a tool in the ideation process to visually predict and explore the user’s experience with the application.

DIGITAL WIREFRAMES

From the sketch prototype, grayscale wireframes were made by the team. From there, the team edited the grayscale wireframes thinking more about UX language and design.


BRANDING AND ILLUSTRATION

Within the project I made illustrations for the coaching pages with the on-boarding process to emphasize the main goals of the application and what the user can achieve.


ISO FINAL SOLUTION

The grayscale wireframes were then turned into a final IOS prototype.
